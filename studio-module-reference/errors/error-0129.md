|------------------------|  
|Number of columns in the dataset exceeds allowed limit.|  
|Number of columns in the dataset in '{0}' exceeds allowed.'|

|Number of columns in the dataset in '{0}' exceeds allowed '{1}' limit of '{2}'.'|
